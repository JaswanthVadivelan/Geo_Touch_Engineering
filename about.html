About Geo Touch Engineering

Delivering geotechnical excellence since 2009

Founded by G.R. Vadivelan in 2009, Geo Touch Engineering has become a symbol of trust and quality in geotechnical services. With expertise in soil nailing, shotcreting, guniting, micropiling, and anchoring, we serve private clients in India.

Our strength lies in innovation, safety, and commitment to quality. Every project reflects our dedication to excellence and client satisfaction.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted geotechnical engineering firm in India, known for pioneering technology and delivering sustainable solutions.

Our Mission

To provide safe, cost-effective, and innovative ground engineering services that meet the highest quality and environmental standards.
